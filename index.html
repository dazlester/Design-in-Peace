Helping Architects & Specifiers Put an End to Nuisance Calls & Emails

Companies such as Glenigan and Barbour ABI sell your contact details & information about the projects you're working on to construction product manufacturers & various service providers - so they can cold call & spam email you!

These services provide no value in return to you, whilst generating millions of pounds in revenue for themselves.

The good news is, they MUST remove your details from their database if you request them to do so. And then so must every other company they have sold that data to (thanks, GDPR!).

Design in Peace exists to make that process quick & easy.

Simply complete our online form and we will submit the required formal requests on your behalf and get you removed from these databases.
